Investigadores principales

Luis Chamba-Eras

Director del grupo

Luis Chamba-Eras (ORCID: http://orcid.org/0000-0003-3069-9628 and ResearcherID: I-6755-2013), professor and researcher from Loja-Ecuador. He's Engineer in Computer Science (UTPL) Master in Computational Engineering and Intelligent Systems (UPV/EHU) Master in Distance Education (UNL). Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of the Basque Country UPV/EHU, the supervisors were Ana Arruarte and Jon A. Elorriaga.
His research interests include: Social Computing, Artificial Intelligence in Education, Computational Trust Models, e-Trust, Virtual Learning Communities and Learning Objects. Now he's a member of the GITIC, GaLan and Tepuy research groups.

Milton Leonardo Labanda-Jaramillo

(Investigador-profesor)

Milton Labanda-Jaramillo (ORCID: http://orcid.org/0000-0001-7370-3901 and ResearcherID: E-), professor and researcher from Loja-Ecuador. He's Engineer in Informatics (UTPL) Master Universitario en Software Libre UOC .
His research interests include: OpenData, BigData, Programming Languages and ITC for Education.
Now he's a member of the GITIC research group.

Edison Leonardo Coronel-Romero

(Investigador-profesor)

Edison Leonardo Coronel-Romero (ORCID: http://orcid.org/0000-0001-5407-9023 and ResearcherID: E-3531-2018), professor and researcher from Loja-Ecuador. He's Engineer in Systems (UNL) Master Universitario en Software Libre .
His research interests include: Systems Development, Business Intelligence, Social Networks, Cloud Computing, Systems Architecture, Information Systems, Programming Languages and New Technologies.
Now he's a member of the GITIC research group.
